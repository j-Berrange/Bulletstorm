**Overview**  
An advanced **3D first-person shooter** featuring a **physics-based gun system** and **node-based animations**. The weapon system includes a **node-based attachment framework** with an **in-game economy** for purchasing guns and attachments. **Ranks and player progress are saved in cloud storage**. The project showcases **camera, character, lighting, and UI animations**, **carefully crafted sound design**, and visual effects such as **light rays**, **depth of field**, and **dynamic color grading**.

**Project Size**  
- **Duration:** 2 years  
- **Code:** 26,000 lines  
- **Comments:** 3,000 lines  

---

## 🔥 Highlights
- ⚙️ **Physics-Driven Gunplay:** Accurate recoil, spread, and handling mapped to a robust node system.  
- 🧩 **Node-Based Attachments:** Modular attachments per weapon
- 💰 **In-Game Economy:** Earn money from matches to buy weapons and attachments.  
- ☁️ **Cloud Persistence:** Player rank, XP, and inventory saved across sessions.  
- 🎬 **Polished Presentation:** Camera/character/lighting/UI animations + curated SFX.  
- 🎨 **Cinematic VFX:** Light rays, depth of field, and dynamic color grading.

---

## 🕹️ Core Mechanics
- The goal is simple: **get kills**.  
- Use **money** to **buy weapons**.  
- Gain **XP** to **level up**.  
  - Leveling up **unlocks new weapons**.  
- **Kills are tracked per weapon**.  
  - More kills on a weapon **unlock more attachments**.
